Sir, why is it 6×a² and not just a²?
Praketa
A cube has six faces, and each face is a square of side a. Each one has area a². Add all six together: a² + a² + a² + a² + a² + a² = 6a². That's why.

Sir is this same for a Rubik's cube?
Praketa
Exactly the same! A Rubik's cube is a cube. If one edge is 5.7 cm, the surface area is 6 × 5.7² ≈ 195 cm². But we usually ignore the small grooves between the squares.
