Frequently asked

Common questions, honest answers.

Are the teachers real people?+

No. Every instructor on Ananta is an AI persona. Lessons are scripted, voiced and animated by AI systems. This is precisely what makes a ₹299 monthly price possible while keeping teaching quality consistent.

How does the AI answer my child's doubts?+

During the live class, a student submits a doubt by text or voice note. The AI instructor references the lesson material and responds within seconds, in the chosen language. The Sunday doubt-clearing session covers anything still unresolved.

What happens on Sunday?+

Sunday has two parts: a one-hour auto-graded weekly test covering everything taught Monday to Saturday, followed by a one-hour live doubt-clearing session where the AI teachers address questions from the week.

What is the Memory Deck?+

The Memory Deck is a personal flashcard collection in the student profile. After each class, Ananta automatically generates revision flashcards from the lesson and adds them to the deck, using spaced repetition to strengthen weak areas before the weekly test.

How do I pay ₹299? Is a credit card required?+

No credit card is needed. Payment is via UPI Auto-Pay — set it up once, and the amount is debited automatically each cycle. It works with every Indian bank.
